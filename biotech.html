Screenprinting Project

During this Screenprinting project we learned various skills that we will use for the rest of our life. Some used more than others but lets get into what this project consisted of and what we had to do to succeed. In this section of the project we had to first start drafting what we wanted to do to implement it into the real world. The purpose of this project was to provide a message for people to see to bring awareness to some sort of issue and or help fix it using some part of our body. Here are the steps we took to accomplish this.

Planning

During this step we had to make a rough draft to start planning what we wanted our project to look like.

Photography

During this step in the project we had to start taking photos of the school approprite body part we wanted to use.

Frame Production via Adobe Illustrator

During this step of the project we used Adobe Illustrator to setup a plan of how big our frame was going to be.

Sawing

During this step we started constructing our frame and cutting the pieces precisely to make our perfect unique frame.

Stapling

In this step we stapled the wood to make our final frame come together into one piece.

Stretching Fabric

During this step we started stretching fabric on the frame so there was no loose edges on the fabric and the ink was able to easily and levely go through.

Cutting Vinyl

In this step we cut vinyl so our image was able to go through the fabric with nothing else but the image appearing after laying ink.

Laying Ink

In the final step; layering ink, we as you could probably guessed.. layed ink ont the finished frame through fabric to get our pristine image out.
